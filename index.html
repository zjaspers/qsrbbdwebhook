Important Notes:

  • Always check for signs of spoilage (odd smell, curdling, mold) before using, regardless of the date.
  • Keep all dairy products refrigerated at 40°F (4°C) or below.
  • Use the "First In, First Out" (FIFO) method when stocking dairy products.
  • For unopened shelf-stable milk alternatives, check the "Best By" date on the package.
  • Once opened, shelf-stable milk alternatives should be treated like refrigerated milk.
  • This checklist should be completed daily as part of opening procedures.
